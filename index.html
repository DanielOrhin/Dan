SALEM BETHEL ASSEMBLY

VARIOUS MINISTRIES IN THE CHURCH AND WHAT THEY STAND FOR

EVANGELISM MINISTRY

EVANGELISM

It is the presentation of Jesus Christ in the power of the Holy Spirit with the view that people will come and trust Jesus as Savior and Lord and Sever him in the fellowship of the church. Evangelism is the responsibilities of every Church member

PEMEM MINISTRY

The men ministry organizes seminars workshops, lectures and symposia on all aspects of life including : Marriage Enriching, husband responsibilities at home, child care and Education, Business management and techniques, leadership and responsibilities in church and the state; current affairs at both national and international levels; the teaching of basic principle of domestic law and other specialized men-related ministries. The men’s ministry has a slogan that goes Man!, the image and the Glory, Man, be strong and courageous, Man, we are firmly established.Also the church have taught about the male youth and brought about a ministry that will raise men of great value. This ministry is called "GENTLE GIANT"

Women Ministry

The Women Ministry organizes seminars workshops, lectures and symposia on all aspects of life including : Marriage Enriching, Wives responsibilities at home, child care and Education, Business management and techniques. The Ladies have a sub ministry with the intention of capturing and raising ladies with much virtue called “VIRTOUS LADIES”

YOUTH MINISTRY

To encourage youths to know God’s word, develop a relationship with Jesus Christ and become active in the church community. The youth ministry of the church of Pentecost comprises all members between the age of 12 and 40. The slogan for Youth is “Youth , Arise and Shine, Youth Remember Your Creator Now, Youth, Empowered to live for Christ Every”

CHILDREN

TThe children’s ministry trains children along biblical precepts of balanced growth. This includes lessons on salvation, Worship and Obedience. It support such training with games, quizzes and sword Drills.
